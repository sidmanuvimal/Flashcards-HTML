Welcome to the FlashCards app!

This web application is designed to help you create, study, and manage your flashcards effectively.

Whether you're preparing for an exam or learning something new, FlashCards provides an easy-to-use interface that lets you:

  • Create Custom Flashcards: Add your own questions and answers to build a personalized collection of flashcards.
  • Manage Flashcards: Edit or delete flashcards directly from the main page to keep your collection organized.
  • Practice Mode: Flip flashcards to reveal answers and test your knowledge interactively.
  • Progress Tracking: Track your correct and incorrect answers with a dynamic progress bar.
  • Shuffle Flashcards: Randomize the order of flashcards to make your practice sessions more challenging.
  • Keyboard Shortcuts: Use the Enter key to quickly add flashcards or navigate between inputs.
  • Responsive Design: Enjoy a seamless experience on both desktop and mobile devices.
  • Interactive Welcome Text: Experience dynamic text that reacts to your mouse movements for a fun and engaging interface.

FlashCards is your ultimate tool for mastering any subject. Start creating your flashcards today and take your learning to the next level!
